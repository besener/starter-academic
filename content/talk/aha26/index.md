summary: "This experimental session will bring together scholars of economic and material history, cultural and religious exchange, anthropology, art history, and archaeology offsite at Chicago’s Field Museum of Natural History to examine a unique display of shipwreck artifacts from the Java Sea. A panel of historians of China, Southeast Asia, Mongol-dominated Eurasia, and the medieval Islamicate world will consider how a multi-disciplinary and multi-regional approach can illuminate the premodern history of Inter-Asian trade. Objects recovered from the twelfth-thirteenth century Java Sea Shipwreck will be on special display for the session and will serve as the centerpiece of reflection and discussion."
